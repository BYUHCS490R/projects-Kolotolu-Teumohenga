What goes in the header?

A logo that identifies the website. It is a banner bar that goes across the top of the page.

What goes in the nav?

Links that direct users to more resources to learn more about the website.

Can the nav go in the header? Does it have to go in the header?

Yes, the nav can go in the header. It can also go above or below the header. On Canvas, you may find the nav on the side of the website. No, the nav does not have to go in the header.

Does the nav contain links to another website?

Yes

What goes in Main?

In the "main" section of a webpage you will find the important information that the company or owner of the webpage wants users to be captivated by. It consists of the most important information.

What goes in the Footer?

Things that go in the footer would be thing that you aren't really highlighting or contact information. You may also see a copyright notice.
