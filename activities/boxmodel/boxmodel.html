What is the Difference between block tags and inline tags?

Block tags form a box horizontally and does not like to share space with anything else while an inline tage selects certain text within a blocktag and only effects that specific selection.

What is the margin?

The margin effects the outside of the box and works on pushing things outside of the box further away from the box.

What is the border?

Like the name may suggest, the border effects the edge of thebox and creates a visible border in which you can change the style size or color.

What is padding?

Padding effects the placing of your text withing the box. It pushes the text inwards away from the edge

What are the units of measurement you can use in CS?

Pixels, percentafe, em, rem, pt, etc.

When would you use a figure tag?

Another block tag that is used to surround an img tag. Used to make the img tag a block tag. Figcaption allows you to have captions within te box.
