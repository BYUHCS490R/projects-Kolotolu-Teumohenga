What kind of photos are good to encode using JPEG?

JPEG is good at encoding real life photos

What kind of photos are good to encode using GIF?

GIFs are good for encoding, logos, icons and buttons. GIFs are good at capturing animations but not very well at capturing real life videos.

What is unique about the PNG format?

While PNGs and GIFs are similar, PNGs have the ability to be transparent.

How do you resize you photo to the size it should be displayed?

You can resize the photo by clicking the three dots on the image and clicking resize image button.

Why should you resize photos using photo editing software instead of resizing it using CSS?

When you have a really big picture it costs less money for those downloading the images if you just use the photoediting software instead of CSS.
